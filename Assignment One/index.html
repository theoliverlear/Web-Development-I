Painting

A painting which I will always hang on my wall is a painting of a an album cover from the music I made. It was called "Hand-Me-Downs" and this painting reminds me of what everyone in my life did to help me through a difficult time. To not be specific, the trauma of my childhood made me very unwell. I was relentlessly bullied until I left my school junior year of high school. My one real friend, who I had known since I was 6, was the only person who would talk to me. I was so lonely and depressed. Over the course of these years, we would play tennis together and watch movies. He's an introvert and I'm an extrovert, so I knew that when said yes to hanging out, that it was a big deal. I was so grateful for him. So this painting by my sister, who is a shy painter but said yes to painting for me, reminds me of the people who were there for me when I needed them most.

Computer

As a developer, something meaningful to me is my computer. I ended up being able to afford it after trading cryptocurrencies in early 2021. In summer of 2021, I fully assembled my computer — which had all the items I'd hoped for. It is a self-built computer and it was initially intended for editing audio as my current job was working as an audio engineer. I also used to to produce my own music . with the computer. I have since moved on to software development and I use it for that now. I am very proud of my computer and the things I can do with it. I'll never forget the first Codecademy class I completed with it. I was so excited to be learning something new and I'm so grateful and lucky that I had a computer to do it on. My computer is a manifestation of my personality where I can create and learn new things by myself — on my own accord.
