Independent Projects

The independent projects I pursue are programming projects that build my skills and knowledge. My goal is to build a professional portfolio to showcase my skills. I work on projects of my choosing. I’ll list a few of my projects. One is a cryptocurrency trader which is meant to automate cryptocurrency transactions with a buy low, sell high algorithm. Another is a program that analyzes the cryptocurrency market and someone’s portfolio using Python’s data science libraries like Matplotlib and pandas. A group I lead at my university is a programming team who works on open-source projects. We are called the UWRF Development Team. The team is working on a currency converter program which gives up-to-date conversions of various currencies and acts as a wallet for investing endeavors. These projects are important on their own, but their development builds my skills, and this hobby is important to me for that reason. I constantly push myself and I believe that my work speaks to that.
