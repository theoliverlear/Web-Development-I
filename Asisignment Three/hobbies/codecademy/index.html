Codecademy

My Codecademy hobby is a daily endeavor. Codecademy is a service for learning programming skills. It teaches various topics. It sets a goal for the user to learn and tracks your progress. Currently, I’ve used Codecademy five days a week for thirty-five weeks – as well as before that point. It is an amazing service that I encourage anyone who is interested in programming to use. It balances two important parts of education: learning and doing. I have taken many classes from Codecademy, some of which include Java, C++, HTML and CSS, JavaScript, Python, and others. Like my other hobbies, my Codecademy hobby builds my skills as a programmer and ultimately will help me be prepared to join the industry.
