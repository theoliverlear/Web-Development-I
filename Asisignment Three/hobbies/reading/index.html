Reading

My reading hobby is one I enjoy very much and spend a lot of time partaking in. I read many books and they tend to be about programming. I use these books to build my skills like my other hobbies. I am a particular fan of Java and so I read many books to learn more about Java – though I read about other topics like functional programming and web development as well. I find reading these books gives me a detailed enhancement to my knowledge that online information does not contextualize as well – StackOverflow for instance. I also read books besides programming books. I am currently reading “American Prometheus” by Kai Bird and Martin J. Sherwin. It is a biography about Julius Robert Oppenheimer. I like to read books like this to educate my worldview. There are some novels I enjoy as well like the Thomas Harris’ Hannibal series. My main goal with reading is to better myself but also enjoy the content I choose to read about.
